Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the website. You will be asked to provide some basic information such as your name, email address, and password.

How do I search for jobs?

You can search for jobs by entering keywords related to the job you are looking for in the search bar on the website. You can also filter your search results by location, job type, salary, and more.

How do I apply for a job?

To apply for a job, click on the "Apply Now"button on the job listing page. You will be asked to provide your resume and cover letter, as well as answer some job-specific questions if applicable.
